//APPLICATION INFORMATION//
  • Purpose: This app allows you to track and export missing size data across various product types by gender.
    •
  • Required Fields: To add an entry, you must fill out Store Name, Product Name, Product Code, Gender, and Product Type.
    •
  • Size Grid: Size options dynamically adjust based on selected gender and product type.
    •
  • Entry List: Entries are sorted by gender and grouped by product type.
    •
  • Size Limitations: Plus sizes are currently not supported.
    •
  • Mobile Use: This app is mobile-friendly and supports touch selection for size buttons.
